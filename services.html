Regulatory

Planning & Licencias

Navigating the Spanish planning system for works requiring a licencia de obras. I coordinate with local aparejadores and partner arquitectos to handle the statutory submission, managing the process on your behalf and explaining each stage in English. The licencia de obras is a mandatory requirement for most renovation works in Spain and must be obtained before works begin.

  • Planning strategy note
  • Coordination with aparejador and partner arquitecto
  • Document preparation and submission management
  • Liaison with the local Concello
  • Progress updates in English throughout
Obra menor (minor works)
€500 / £428

Internal works, repairs, window/door replacements. Includes coordination with aparejador.

Obra mayor (major works)
€900 / £770

Full rehabilitation, structural works, extensions. Arquitecto fees are additional and quoted separately.
