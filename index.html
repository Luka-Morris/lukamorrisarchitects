Current project · Negreira, A Coruña, Galicia

Casa Negreira

A full rehabilitation of a derelict rural stone house in Negreira, Galicia. The project is both my own home and a live demonstration of the practice's approach to rural Galician architecture. Two-storey granite rubble masonry building, partially collapsed internally.

  • Terracotta tilesHandmade terracotta floor tiles throughout the ground floor — connecting to Galician vernacular.
  • Double-height with skylightNew roof aperture over the principal living space, drawing light into the deep-plan masonry structure.
  • Retained timber beamOriginal timber ridge beam preserved — datum between old fabric and new intervention.
  • Exposed stone wallsInternal stone faces cleaned and repointed with lime mortar. No render, no insulation board.
  • Wall lightsWarm wall-mounted lighting against bare stone — atmospheric, residential light.
